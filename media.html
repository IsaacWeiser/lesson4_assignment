3d printing Filaments
Name Pros Cons
PLA Cheap, dimensional accuracy, easy prep Low melting point, brittle filaments, tends to ooze
ASA Unaffected by sunlight, can withstand pressure, and heat resistance Expensive, requires very high tempature to print, printing process emits toxic fumes
PETG Smooth surface, less suceptible to water damage Poor structural integrity, tendency for threads to crack
ABS Cheap, Strong, heat resistant Heavy warping, odorous printing, pieces tend to shrink
Nylon Can withstand pressure, flexible Warp prone, absorbs water, poor performance in moist conditions
